November 19, 2016

When: Saturday, November 19, 2016

Time: 4:00 PM to 7:00 PM (PST)

WHERE: Bayview Opera House 4705 3rd St, San Francisco, California 94124

Evangelist Wanda Peyton-Thomas has the role of Preacher and School Counselor

Clown In The Prayer Closet takes you on a mental journey of life, puppy love, sex, drugs,and dysfunctional family life told through the eyes of a sixteen year old young lady named Denise. A great Stage Play with some great life lessons. Our headliners are Suga T sister of E40 and an original member of the Clique we are also glad to present T-Millz from P Diddy's Making The Band. This play has no harsh language, violence or sexual content.

Find out more at www.LisaB.Online or call (707)720-7671

Doors open at 3:30 PM
