The Ministry of Wanda M. Thomas

Wanda M. Thomas Ministries was founded on the belief that communities must learn to help themselves. Our work is guided by the following five pillars of faith:

  • A duty to reach out to the less fortunate
  • An obligation to lead by example
  • An all-inclusive approach: We help everyone regardless of their religious beliefs
  • Acceptance of the fact that we, too, may someday require assistance in our time of need
  • The path to salvation lies on our faith in our Savior
    • read more

    Our Mission

    Our goal is to encourage, inspire, and uplift those who have suffered hurts during their life’s experiences. We help people to examine themselves, identify, and understand past hurts, which ultimately hinder their life’s progress.

    We host an annual “Sister I’m Sorry Conference”. Using the powerful tool of forgiveness, we strive to break the cycle of pain to start the healing process.

    The Power Of Forgiviness

    Wanda M Thomas has a life story that has been filled with much hurt and pain. On this journey she has learned several life lessons dealing with the Awesome Power of Forgiveness. She has through various settings shared her testimony candidly as a teaching tool. She has used her experiences to help others through the healing process of forgiveness.

    She reveals the path she has had to take helping others to understand as we forgive God forgives us and elevates us into a higher place of freedom and love.

    Virtuous Women and Mighty Men of Valor United Ministries

    This ministry is the brainchild of Evangelist Wanda Thomas birthed by her desire to unite men and women in ministry. This is a group comprised of Godly men and women who are warriors for the kingdom of God. Our group includes singers, worshipers, musicians, poets, actors and actresses, praise dancers, counselors, preachers, teachers, intercessors, prayer warriors, and prophets.

    In order to reach the masses we must be versatile using all the spiritual gifts given by God. James 1:17 Every good gift and every perfect gift is from above, and cometh down from the Father.

    The Word of God Says Write the Vision...

    Evangelist Wanda Thomas has been in Ministry since her early twenties doing what God has commissioned her to do. Reaching out to the hurting in communities throughout the United States and in other countries.

    This ministry seek out those who are in need because of negative circumstances that may have caused hurts and pain. We show the love of Christ by ministering healing, showing unconditional love, teaching the Word, giving tools that will bring hope for a better future with Christ as the head of their lives

    Danny Thomas

    Evangelist Wanda Thomas is currently employed by American National Insurance where she has achieved 5th place status in the company nationwide. She is the mother of four sons, Cardel, Stuart, Marlas, and An and is married to mastor keyboardist Danny A. Thomas, song writer, composer, producer, R&B Hall of Fame Inductee, and Original Member of the legendary group Con Funk Shun. They met when Evangelist Wanda Thomas was an invited speaker at the church where brother Thomas was minister of music. In Brother Thomas own words "I fell in love with her ministry before I fell in love with her." A Ruth and Boaz story. They have been working together as a ministry team every since.
