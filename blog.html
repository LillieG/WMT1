The Word

  • By Wanda
  • Posted on September 5, 2016 at 10:00 PM

God's Word says in Luke 6:38 Give, and it shall be given unto you; good measure, pressed down, and shaken together, and running over, shall men give into your bosom. For with the same measure that ye mete withal it shall be measured to you again.

When we hear this scripture we automatically think of money, but this scripture can be applied to other things beside money.

What stands out is, “it shall be measured to you again”. Which means whatever you put out comes back to you in most cases DOUBBLE!!

So how about giving a helping hand, a smile, a kind word or two. You get back what you give out!! Do that today.
