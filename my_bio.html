WANDA M. THOMAS


Theme Scripture: II Corinthians 10:4

For the weapons of our warfare are not carnal, but mighty through God to the pulling down of strongholds.

Wanda M. Thomas is a woman of excellence who retired from Wells Fargo Bank of California and American General Life and Accident Company.

She is the Pastor of Grace Deliverance Ministries, an international ministry was established in Tampa Florida. Her purpose is to unite men and women in ministry, therefore, Virtuous Women and Mighty Men of Valor United Ministries was birthed. Evangelist Thomas accepted the Lord as her personal Savior and received the baptism of the Holy Spirit at the age of 12. She acknowledged her called into the ministry in her early 20’s.

Evangelist Thomas is an intercessor with a primary focus on deliverance. She is a worshiper, often quoting Psalms 34:8 "Oh taste and see that the Lord is good; blessed is the man that trusteth in him."
